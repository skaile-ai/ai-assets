# Narrative Arcs

## Partner Deck

**Audience:** Consulting firms / consultants (McKinsey alumni, Bain alumni, mid-tier)
**Emotional journey:** Curiosity → Recognition → Excitement → Urgency

| Beat               | Slide(s) | Emotion     | Message                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------ | -------- | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1. The Question    | 1-2      | Recognition | "Every client is asking you: 'How do we leverage AI?' You need a better answer than chatbots."                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 2. The Gap         | 3-4      | Frustration | "The tools exist but they're all wrong: prototypes (bolt.new), vendor lock-in (Mendix), developer-only (Cursor), or niche single-purpose tools (Harvey, Gong) that create tool sprawl. Every category fails for the same reason — none let the business user execute structured AI processes at enterprise grade." |
| 3. The Unlock      | 5-7      | "Aha!"      | "Saxe lets your consultants — who already understand the client's business — turn that knowledge into real enterprise apps. And the same platform can power your own consulting delivery: research → recommendation → presentation. AI does the heavy lifting, the consultant provides judgment, IT governs it."   |
| 4. How It Works    | 8-10     | Confidence  | "Describe → Concept → Build → Deploy. Live demo / walkthrough of the pipeline. Show the three-panel workspace."                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 5. Why It's Real   | 11-12    | Trust       | "Built on a proprietary framework — data2impact has shipped 10 production apps for pharma, banking, biotech. Not a prototype. Real code, real deployments, real enterprises."                                                                                                                                      |
| 6. The Opportunity | 13-14    | Excitement  | "New service line: AI-powered app building for clients — plus structured AI pipelines for your own consulting delivery. You bring the domain expertise, Saxe brings the platform. Deployment & customization revenue ($10-200K per client)."                                                                       |
| 7. Why Now         | 15       | Urgency     | "Early partners shape the product. Preferred pricing. First-mover in your market. We're starting conversations this week."                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 8. The Ask         | 16       | Action      | "Try the prototype. Pick one client problem. Let's build it together in a session."                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |

## Enterprise Deck

**Audience:** Business managers at mid-size companies (50–1,000 employees), with CIO backup slides
**Emotional journey:** Anxiety → Hope → Understanding → Confidence

| Beat                   | Slide(s) | Emotion              | Message                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ---------------------- | -------- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1. The Pressure        | 1-2      | Anxiety              | "Your CEO is asking 'What's our AI strategy?' All you have is ChatGPT and Copilot."                                                                                                                            |
| 2. The Real Problem    | 3-4      | Recognition          | "Your team's best ideas are trapped in spreadsheets. IT can't build fast enough. AI tools produce prototypes, not real apps."                                                                                  |
| 3. A New Way           | 5-6      | Hope                 | "What if your people could turn their expertise into real results — apps, deliverables, offers — through AI-guided processes, governed by IT?"                                                                 |
| 4. How It Works        | 7-9      | Understanding        | "Describe your idea (or upload your Excel) → AI guides you through structured phases → review and approve each step → get a real app, presentation, or deliverable. Same platform, different skill templates." |
| 5. IT Stays in Control | 10-11    | Confidence           | "Your auth. Your design system. Your infrastructure. Your AI provider. Every app is real code in your git repo."                                                                                               |
| 6. Proof               | 12-13    | Trust                | "data2impact's technology runs in production at [references]. Enterprise-grade: regulated industries, compliance-ready."                                                                                       |
| 7. The Path            | 14       | Clarity              | "Pilot program: pick 3 shadow Excel apps → convert them in weeks → measure impact."                                                                                                                            |
| 8. The Ask             | 15       | Action               | "Let's identify your first three apps. 30-minute workshop."                                                                                                                                                    |
| CIO Detour             | +3-4     | Technical confidence | Architecture diagram, security model, deployment options, no lock-in guarantee                                                                                                                                 |
