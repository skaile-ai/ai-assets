## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for a comorbidity chapter:
- Include Odds Ratios for each comorbidity relative to the general population
- Include a clear framework for distinguishing comorbidity from differential diagnosis
- Address treatment sequencing (which condition takes priority?)
- Flag drug-drug interactions if multiple medications are needed
- Include the developmental perspective (comorbidity patterns change with age)
