## Rules for Synthesis
- Ground all discussions in the research context files (`_background.md`, `_paper_description.md`, etc.).
- The expert host should guide the technical breakdown.
- The novice host should ground the conversation in practical "so what?" questions.
- Dialectical goal: Explain BOTH the what (data) and the how (logic) of the paper.
