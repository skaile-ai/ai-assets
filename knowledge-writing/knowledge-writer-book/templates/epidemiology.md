## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for an epidemiology chapter:
- Include data from meta-analyses over single studies where possible
- Include national (DACH) data alongside international figures
- If prevalence estimates vary widely, explain the range and reasons
- Include the source study's design and sample size in parentheses
- Address potential diagnostic biases (e.g., underdiagnosis in girls)
