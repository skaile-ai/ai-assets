## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for a diagnosis chapter:
- Include exact ICD-11 codes and criteria
- Include prevalence data with source and year
- Include at least 3 differential diagnoses with distinguishing features
- Include at least 2 validated diagnostic instruments
- Flag uncertain or emerging data points with `[CHECK]`
