## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for an etiology chapter:
- Include heritability estimates with study type and confidence interval
- Include replicated genetic findings only; mark unreplicated with `[CHECK]`
- Always integrate across biological, psychological, and social levels
- Include a diagram-ready integrative model
- Address developmental aspects (pediatric etiology ≠ adult etiology)
- Distinguish correlation from causation explicitly
