## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for a neurotransmitter chapter:
- Include receptor subtypes with their known clinical relevance
- Include development-specific aspects (pediatric pharmacology differs from adult)
- Reference neuroimaging findings with study type and sample size
- Distinguish established from speculative neurobiological models
- Link every biological mechanism to a clinical or therapeutic implication
