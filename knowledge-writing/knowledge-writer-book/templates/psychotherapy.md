## Scaffold Guidance

When building the Knowledge Scaffold for a psychotherapy chapter:
- Include at least 2 manualized programs with their evidence base
- Include a meta-analytic effect size where available
- Include age-specific adaptations (this is child/adolescent psychiatry!)
- Include the guideline recommendation grade (Empfehlungsgrad A/B/0)
- Address combination with pharmacotherapy explicitly
